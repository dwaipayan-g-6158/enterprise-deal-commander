# Credits & Acknowledgements

## The project

**Enterprise Deal Commander (EDC)** is a presales deal-intelligence cockpit built around the
ManageEngine **AD360 / Log360** sales motion. Product direction is captured in the Phase 1 and
Phase 2 PRDs and the improvement proposals under [`product/`](./product/).
